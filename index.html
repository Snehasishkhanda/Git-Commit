Team XSpark

Team XSpark is led by a popular player/streamer Sc0utOP. It stands as a powerhouse in the Indian gaming scene solidifying their status as one of the most formidable teams in the competitive BGMI circuit, celebrated for their extraordinary progress in Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI), with triumphs in both: BGIS 2024 (Battlegrounds Mobile India Series) and BMPS 2024 (Battlegrounds Mobile Pro Series).
